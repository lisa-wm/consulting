BASICS OF NLP & SENTIMENT ANALYSIS

- Tokenization. Breaking down a document into atomic pieces (--> words)

- Part-of-speech tagging. Classifying tokens as verbs, nouns, ...
  --> Requires large dictionaries of pre-tagged texts
  --> Basis for many NLP tasks

- Sentiment analysis. Assigning each sentiment-bearing piece of text a positive / negative / neutral tag + sentiment score
  --> Also requires pre-scored dictionary!
